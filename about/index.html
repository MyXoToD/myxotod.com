About

Hello stranger, my name is Max and I am a frontend & backend web-developer based in Mayence, Germany.

I was born in 1993 in Koblenz, a city in the east-west of Germany. While growing up I moved a lot between small villages near my birth town and Frankfurt am Main. After my graduation at school I made a 3 years education at a small web agency, it was all about websites and webshops. I created my first website in 2005 but I really started to do this professional in 2009. Almost all my work is responsive and compatible to a lot of devices and platforms. My goal is to write clean source code, create self speaking designs and write texts which are worth reading.

2013 I finished my education and moved to Mayence. Since then I work for a small web agency in the middle of the city called Opoloo.
